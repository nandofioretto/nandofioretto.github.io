2019

  • Dec, 2019: Our paper "Predicting AC Optimal Power Flows: Combining Deep Learning and Lagrangian Dual Methods" has been accepted at the Thirty-Fourth AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI-20). We show how to combine Deep Learning and Lagrangian Dual Methods for predicting AC Optimal Power Flows in Energy Networks. The proposed work improves the accuracy of standard linear relaxations by at least two orders of magnitude. Joint work with Pascal Van Hentenryck and Terrence W.K. Mak.

  • Sep, 2019: Our work on privacy-preserving power systems has been accepted for publication in the IEEE Transaction on Power Systems, 2019. Joint work with Pascal Van Hentenryck and Terrence W.K. Mak. We show how to release power system data that is (1) differentially private, and (2) when used for a relevant optimization problem, ensures the existence of a feasible solution with high fidelity. Read More!
  • Aug, 2019: Our work on preserving the security and privacy of several parameters of a power network has been accepted for publication in the IEEE Transaction on Smart Grids, 2019. Joint work with Pascal Van Hentenryck and Terrence W.K. Mak. Our work uses Differential Privacy to protect the re-identification of critical power network parameters, such as those linked to the transmission lines. Read More!
